Take back the wall.

Mantelpiece is an art subscription service that lets you discover local art and artists. Mantelpiece is for the curious, the thoughtful, and the engaged.

How it works

1

The Artists

Our curators find young, up-and-coming artists in the area and invite them to join our catalog. Artists are given generous stipends to help them do what they love.

2

The Patrons

You're on the search for art. When you create an account on Mantelpiece you become a patron, supporting the artists. Every two months, you choose up to four pieces from our catalog and curate your own gallery. Our curators deliver and install the work in your home.

3

The Art

After your gallery has been up for two months, you can choose to buy one or more of your pieces. We pick up the others, then you choose more work to hang. With over 5,000 pieces in every medium from drawing to sculpture, it's the easiest way to discover the art you love.
