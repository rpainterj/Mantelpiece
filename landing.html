How it works

The Artists

Our Curators find young, up-and-coming artists in the area and invite them to become Mantelpiece Artists. Artists are given generous stipends to help them do what they love.The curators maintain an online Catalog of artist work with over 3,000 artists and 10,000 pieces.

Meet the Artists >

The Patrons

You're on the search for art. When you create an account on Mantelpiece you become a Patron, supporting the artists. Every two months, you choose up to four pieces from our catalog that you want in your house. Our curators deliver and install the work in your home. This becomes your Gallery.

Meet the Patrons >

Your Gallery

After your gallery has been up for two months, you can choose to buy one or more of your pieces. We pick up the others, then you pick more work from the catalog to hang. With new art always on your walls, you can build your own art collection within no time.
